You are in the middle of the Otter Express and the Dining Commons!

On the right is the Otter Express, on the left is the Dining Commons

The Otter Express and Dining Commons are two of the dining services offered on campus. The Otter Express features two highly popular and exceptionally delicious dining concepts: WOW Cafe and Sub-connection. In addition, the Otter Express has an expanded Simply-to-Go section to satisfy the busy student who needs to eat on the run. The Dining Commons is food court that can satisfy those hunger pains. Once students pay for their entry in, students have access to an all you can eat food court.
